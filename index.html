1 To start, press the Play Game button. 2 Each player will choose a response key to attack the other player upon the end time of the song played for each round. Player 1 input: Spacebar Player 2 input: K. 3 The players should watch the countdown timer and prepare to respond as close to the songs end as possible 4 When the song ends, both players hit their buttons 5 The game will calculate the reaction times of each player, and display their results. 6 The player with the fastest reaction time scores a point. Repeat steps 2 - 6 until a winner is determined.
