Flash-Act

Instructions

Hover to see instructions!

  • Player 1 input: Spacebar
  • Player 2 input: K.
  1. The mute button will mute all sound for the game until the page is refreshed, and the button is clicked again.
  2. To start, press the Play Game button.
    3. Each player will choose a response key to attack the other player upon the end time of the song played for each round.
  3. The players should watch the countdown timer and prepare to respond as close to the songs end as possible
  4. When the song ends, both players hit their buttons
  5. The game will calculate the reaction times of each player, and display their results.
  6. The player with the fastest reaction time scores a point. Repeat steps 1 - 5 until a winner is determined.
